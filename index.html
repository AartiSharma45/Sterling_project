Water Bottling Plant

A water bottling plant is a facility where water is processed, purified, and packaged into bottles for distribution and sale. The plant typically includes equipment for water treatment (like filtration and sterilization), bottling lines for filling and sealing bottles, and systems for labeling and packaging.

Alkaline Water Import

The process of bringing in alkaline water, which has a higher pH level than regular water, often containing minerals like calcium and magnesium. This water is imported due to perceived health benefits, including balancing body acidity, improving hydration, and providing antioxidants.
